Hello! I am a research fellow in the Machine Teaching group at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS), advised by Dr. Adish Singla.

I graduated with a Bachelor's in Computer Science in the summer of 2024, from the Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) Pilani, India. As a student, I have interned previously with the same group at MPI. I have also interned as a software developer at multiple companies including Google.

I am broadly interested in the design and analysis of intelligent and trustworthy systems, particularly across the areas of AI, programming languages, and human-AI collaboration.
